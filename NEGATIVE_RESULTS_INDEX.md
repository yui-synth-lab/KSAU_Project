- **Status:** **CLOSED (Mathematical Impossibility)**
- **Reason:**

  - **Conclusion:** $q_{mult}=7$ is a **Free Parameter** of the theory, not a derived geometric constant.

### 1.2 Geometric Derivation of Fine Structure Constant ($\alpha_{em}$)
- **Hypothesis:** $\alpha_{em}$ can be derived purely from lattice geometry (e.g., $\pi/432$).
- **Status:** **REJECTED (Statistical False Positive)**
- **Reason:** Monte Carlo simulations reveal a False Positive Rate (FPR) of **87%**. The density of simple geometric fractions is too high to distinguish a true signal from numerology.

### 1.3 Algebraic Map $Co_0 	o G_2$
- **Hypothesis:** There exists a direct algebraic homomorphism or subgroup relation between the Leech automorphism group $Co_0$ and the exceptional Lie group $G_2$.
- **Status:** **REJECTED (Group Theory Mismatch)**
- **Reason:** ATLAS of Finite Groups confirms no such maximal subgroup or representation exists. $G_2$ cannot be embedded into $Co_0$ in a way that generates the particle spectrum.

### 1.4 Compactification Dimension ($D_{bulk\_compact} = 7$)

- **Reason:** Defined as $11 (	ext{M-theory}) - 4 (	ext{Spacetime}) = 7$. This is an input assumption, not an output prediction.
